## I Want the app to:

* Remember what i grab or when i change the focus
  * Single Ancestry
  * Flag that says focus or grab or grab only
* Changing the grab (without changing focus) creates a new entry
* Go backwards and forwards through history
  * backwards means either ungrab, grab only or become focus
  * forward means either grab or grab only or become focus
* History is circular (after the last is the first)
* Remove current recent by typing “/”
* Unlimited length
* Mode to ignore all grabs
